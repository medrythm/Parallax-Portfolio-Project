Project Inspiration

I am Mfumu Wealth Mabunda, residing in Soweto in Johannesburg. I have always been a passionate front-end development student dating back from 2019, eager to enhance my skills and leave a mark on the digital world. However, i often found my self yearning for inspiration and a project that would truly challenge my abilities.

One day, as i was scrolling through my social media feed, i stumbled upon a mesmerising video showcasing the stunning effects of a parallax scrolling effect on a website. intrigued by the visually captivating experience, i delved deeper into the world of parallax websites, reading articles and watching tutorials that explained the intricacies of this innovative web design technique.

With every new piece of information, my curiosity grew, and i knew that i had found the inspiration i had been searching for. Determined to master parallax web development, i set out to create my own project that would not only showcase my skills but also push the boundries of my creativity.

My idea began to take shape as i envisioned a website for a portfolio project for Holberton School. Driven by this newfound inspiration, i tirelessly worked on this project. During my SE programme at ALX i delved into the depths of HTML, CSS and Javascript, mastering the necessary coding techniques to bring my vision to life. Layer by layer, i carefully crafted the website, selecting captivating images and designing captivating animations that would seamlessly guide users through the adventure.

Days turned into nights as i poured my heart and soul into the project. I faced challenges along the way, encountering technical issues and refining my design to ensure a flawless user experience. However, my determination never wavered, and i eagerly embarked each obstacle as a opportunity for growth.
